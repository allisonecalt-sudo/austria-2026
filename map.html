The geography

Salzburg is the hub for both options. Option A keeps you inside the inner ring (everything ≤90 min). Option B reaches out east (Hallstatt) and south (Bled, Slovenia).

Option A — radius from Salzburg

All seven anchor points fit on one Google Map. Sleep stays in Salzburg every night.

🧭 Stops: Salzburg (base) · Königssee (40 min south) · Werfen / Eisriesenwelt (50 min south) · Hallstatt (1h15m east via Bad Ischl) · Wolfgangsee (45 min east) · Liechtensteinklamm (50 min south) · Grossglockner road (1h30m south, day trip)

Option B — three anchors

Salzburg → Hallstatt area → Bled. Border crossing between Austria and Slovenia via Karawanks tunnel (15-30 min wait).

🧭 Salzburg → Gosausee → Hallstatt (1h45 with stop) → Lake Bled (3h15 with border) → Krimml Falls (3h back from Bled) → Salzburg (2h)
