Plane lands ~09:00. Apartment check-in is 14:00-15:00. That's a ~5-hour gap with bags in the car — don't try to fight it, use it. Below are 6 options sorted light → fuller. Pick on the day based on how you feel. You can stack any 2-3 of them.

Hard stop: back at apartment by 17:30-18:00 to cook Shabbat (candle-lighting 20:35). If you do the fortress, plan to be off the rock by 16:00.

First step

Park the car, leave the bags

Salzburg Old Town is pedestrian — you don't drive into it. Park once, walk everything. Both garages are covered + 24h + ~5-min walk to Getreidegasse. Bags stay in the trunk all morning (don't leave anything visible on seats).

Altstadtgarage Mönchsberg

Hildmannplatz 1 · €18-22/day · 24h covered

The classic Old Town garage — carved into the cliff. Take the elevator up and you're on top of Mönchsberg with a 5-min walk down to Getreidegasse. Best if you might do the Museum der Moderne or want the dramatic top-of-the-rock arrival.

The menu · 6 options, mix and match

What's in walking distance

Lightest

1 · Old Town wander

Free · 1-3 hours flex · all flat · 0 min from garage

Getreidegasse (the wrought-iron-sign shopping street), Domplatz (cathedral square), Residenzplatz (with the fountain), Alter Markt (oldest square). UNESCO heritage, dense in 6 blocks, easy to sit on a bench whenever. No tickets, no schedule. The default "we're tired but here we are" plan.

Light

2 · Mirabell Gardens + Salzach river walk

Free · 45-90 min · all flat · 10 min walk from garage (or 5 min from Linzergasse side)

The "Do-Re-Mi" steps from Sound of Music, formal Baroque garden, Pegasus fountain, Hohensalzburg view across the river. Pair with a walk along the Salzach river to Makartsteg (the love-lock bridge) — that's where most postcards happen. Restful, scenic, zero stairs. Walking distance to Chabad / Linzergasse — you could even do this last on the way to dropping bags.

Medium

3 · Mozart Geburtshaus

€13.50 · ~45 min visit · indoor · 2 min walk from garage on Getreidegasse 9

The actual house where Mozart was born (1756), now a 3-floor museum with his violin, a childhood clavichord, family portraits, and listening stations. Compact, well-curated, entirely indoor (good if weather turns). Allison flagged this as a possible draw. Combo-ticket option (€18) adds Mozart-Wohnhaus across the river if you're keeping momentum.

Medium

4 · Hohensalzburg Fortress + funicular

€16.60 all-in (funicular + entry) · 1.5-2 hours · panoramic · must be off the rock by 16:00 for Shabbat prep

The fortress that looms over the city. The €16.60 ticket covers the funicular up (Festungsbahn from Festungsgasse), entry to the courtyards, the Princes' Chambers (Fürstenzimmer), the Magic Theatre, and the panoramic terrace. The funicular saves the climb — you're at the top in 1 minute. Views are the best in Salzburg. If you skip interior rooms, you can do this in 60-75 min total.

Fuller

5 · Mondsee on the way to the apartment

Free (parking ~€3/hr) · ~1 hour stop · 25-30 min detour east of Salzburg

Storybook lake village on the way out of Salzburg toward Salzkammergut — but for Friday it's a quick day-trip detour. Park at the lakefront, walk to the basilica (the wedding scene from Sound of Music), 10-min lakeside stroll, ice cream by the water, back in the car. Best if you land energized + want lake-air-not-cobblestone. Adds ~50 min total to driving. Be back in Salzburg by 16:30 latest.

6 · Werfen castle on the way south

€18 castle (no funicular ride for the falconry show today — Friday schedule constraint) · ~2 hours · 40 min south of Salzburg

Hohenwerfen Fortress (where they filmed "Where Eagles Dare") sits dramatically above the Salzach valley. Cliffside views, falconry show daily 11:15 + 15:15. The 15:15 show is risky for Friday — by the time you drive back, it's 17:00+ and tight. The 11:15 show works if you go straight there from the airport. Skip this Friday and save it for a Tuesday-Wednesday day-trip — but listed here as a "if energy is huge" option. Ice cave (Eisriesenwelt) is separate + a half-day commitment — not Friday material.

Sample stacks

If you want a pre-baked plan

🌿 Lightest day

Park 09:45 → Old Town wander → café (see Cafés page) → bench break → Mirabell Gardens → walk to Linzergasse apartment 14:00. 2-3 hours of activity with long café breaks in the middle.

🥾 Medium day

Park 09:45 → Mozart Geburtshaus 10:15 → Old Town wander → café lunch break (own food + specialty coffee) → Mirabell Gardens → Linzergasse 14:00. ~4 hours active, 2 hours breaks.

⛰️ Fuller day

Park 09:45 → Mozart Geburtshaus → fortress funicular up at 11:30 → top of fortress 13:30 → down by 14:30 → café break → Mirabell quick → home by 16:30. Tight but feasible.

🚗 Day-trip flavor

Skip the city — drive 35 min east → Mondsee village, lake walk, basilica → back via the apartment for a 13:30 early check-in attempt → settle in unhurried. Quietest, most air, the choice if you arrive over-stimulated.
