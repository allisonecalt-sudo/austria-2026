First leg · Shabbat stay

Anchor the Shabbat. Three apartment options, current lean Glücksmomente (Bad Goisern) — all Jul 24-26, 2 nights, free cancellation, none cancelled. Because food is cooked in the apartment, a stovetop is the deciding amenity. (This first leg is the only base still being decided — Zell, Gosau, and the airport night are all booked.)

Shabbat Nachamu · Parashat Vaetchanan. Candle-lighting Fri 20:35 · Havdalah Sat 21:49 (Geonim 8.5°). Food: brought from Israel, cooked in the apartment — no kosher groceries or restaurants in the region. Synagogue: Chabad of Salzburg (Linzer Gasse 76) and the IKG synagogue (Lasserstraße 8) are both an option Shabbat morning, not a must.
