Why DiscoverCars and not Booking?

Avital flagged Booking's car search bounced her to wrong destinations. DiscoverCars has the same supplier inventory with cleaner filtering, sometimes cheaper. The "Full Coverage" zero-excess product on DiscoverCars also tends to be cheaper than Booking's equivalent for the same supplier.

⚠ Deposit hold reality — bring the right card

Every supplier puts a credit-card hold at pickup: roughly €800 for a compact, up to €2,500 for a compact SUV with Sixt Premium Cover waiver. It's released after return + inspection (3-10 days). Bring a credit card with at least €2k free headroom — debit cards are refused at Europcar SZG and unreliable at others.

The Austria-specific addendum (vignette, IDP, alcohol, fuel, debit-card gotcha)
Vignette · €12.80 for 10 days Austrian motorways require a digital vignette (linked to the plate, not the windscreen). Most SZG rentals include it — confirm at the counter. If yours doesn't, buy a 10-day digital vignette at asfinag.at before driving onto the A1 / A10. The Walserberg → Salzburg-Nord stretch (airport-to-old-town) is vignette-exempt.
International Driving Permit (IDP) Allison drives on an Israeli license. Austria + Germany both recommend an IDP alongside. Free from MEMSI in Israel — pick it up before the trip. Most counters rent without it, but it removes a refusal risk.
Alcohol · 0.05% BAC limit Austria + Germany both: 0.05% BAC for licensed drivers (≥2 years). One glass of wine for a 60kg adult is roughly ceiling. Pick the return-driver before opening the bottle at sunset.
Fuel · ask for full-to-full Full-to-empty looks cheaper on paper, but the per-litre markup is brutal. Last gas station before SZG on return is on the B1 / A1 ring road, ~3 min before the rental pavilion.
Germany cross-border · default-allowed The German border is 2 km from the SZG rental desks; cross-border to Berchtesgaden / Königssee is default-allowed on SZG rentals. Confirm at the counter and get it on the contract before driving (especially if you swap a day for Berchtesgaden). No German Maut for passenger cars; no LEZ in Berchtesgaden.
Debit card warning · Europcar SZG Europcar Salzburg accepts credit cards only — no debit. Verify Allison's card type before pickup day. Sixt / Hertz / Alamo / Avis are debit-OK in most car classes (varies by class — check at booking).
"Or similar" caveat Every aggregator booking is for a vehicle CLASS, not a specific model. "Opel Mokka or similar" can deliver a Hyundai Bayon, Peugeot 2008, Kia Stonic, or Renault Captur. Going Sixt-direct VW Golf buys model certainty if it matters.
Pickup arrival photo · 5 sec, saves disputes At pickup, take phone photos of the fuel gauge + odometer + a quick walk-around with timestamps. Same at return. If the counter is closed at 20:00 (key-drop), this is your only evidence against phantom-damage claims.
