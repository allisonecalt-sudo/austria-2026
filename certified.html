Certified — the easy way

Products carrying a real hechsher that are sold in ordinary Austrian supermarkets — Billa, Spar, Hofer, Interspar, MPreis. No label-reading, no judgment calls. This is your normal standard, so start here.

Two rules for this page. ① Where it says “needs the stamp”, the product is only kosher with the printed hechsher symbol on that pack — same brand without the symbol doesn't count. ② Every entry is from Misrachi Austria's 2023 edition and its validity dates have expired, so treat this as what is normally certified and confirm against the symbol on the carton or the live guide.
