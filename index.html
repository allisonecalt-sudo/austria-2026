⭐ The peak

The Dachstein in the water.

Vorderer Gosausee — the most-photographed lake in Austria for a reason. The Dachstein glacier mirrors in the still water at the end of the valley. Flat 1-hour gravel loop, mostly empty after the day-tripper buses leave at 17:00. From our apartment door at Der Ulmenhof, ten minutes. Sunset on the shore · 20:51.

Two sunsets here (Tue + Wed). The you-will-stand-still moment of the trip.

↓
⛰️ Wed Jul 29 · day trip

5 Fingers over Hallstättersee.

Wednesday is a full day from the Gosau base — the Dachstein-Krippenstein cable car valley station is twenty-five minutes away. Two gondola stages climb to 2,109 m. The 5 Fingers cantilevered viewing platform juts out over Hallstättersee. Hallstatt Markt is fifteen minutes the other direction — the famous lakefront walk. Both in one day, both home for sunset at the apartment by 20:00.

Alternative day-trip picks from the same base: Hinterer Gosausee (deeper valley hike), Dachstein ice cave, or a slow recovery day on the Vorderer Gosausee out the apartment door.

Why not overnight at the summit? Avital counter-proposed simplifying — the Wed-night summit stay was dropped in favour of a full day-trip from the Gosau base.
