⭐ The peak

The last electric boat back.

Königssee is the only lake in Germany without combustion engines — silent since 1909. We catch the late boat from St. Bartholomä. Watzmann's east wall goes gold over our right shoulder. The lake goes silver. Sunset on the water · 20:50.

This is the you-will-stand-still moment of the trip. Hours, not minutes.

↓
⛰️ The summit night

Sleep on the Dachstein plateau.

Wednesday afternoon we pack one small overnight bag and take the Dachstein cable car up to Lodge am Krippenstein — 2,063 m, modern architectural lodge on the plateau, Zirben-pine beds. We drop the bag and walk fifteen minutes to the 5 Fingers platform — five steel fingers cantilevered straight over the cliff — and watch the sun set across Hallstättersee while the Dachstein massif goes pink behind us.

The last cable car down leaves at 19:10. When it does, the day-trippers go with it. We stay. Dinner BYO at the lodge, stars without light pollution, sunrise over the Dachstein at 05:30, first car down at 08:40.

One night only — the trip splurge. Locked Wed Jul 29. Same Obertraun valley as our base — zero routing detour.
