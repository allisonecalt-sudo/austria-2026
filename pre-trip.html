⚠ Week-of re-verify checklist (do T-7 days)

Trip is ~2 months out. These 7 things can change between May and July — re-check the week before you leave. Most can be confirmed in 30 minutes. Don't skip 1-4 (peak-moment dependencies).

  1. Königssee last electric-boat departure (Tue Jul 28) — the peak Tara-tier moment. If the boat schedule shifts, the day breaks. Re-verify via Bayerische Seenschifffahrt (2026 timetable should be published by spring).
  2. Schafbergspitze cog LAST UP departure (Wed Jul 29) — you need to catch the last cog up to sleep at the summit. Miss it and there's no summit night. Confirm via Schafbergbahn timetable.
  3. Eisriesenwelt hours + reservation policy (Thu Jul 30) — already flagged as may be too strenuous (1,400 stairs). Confirm walk-in vs reservation policy + your appetite that day. eisriesenwelt.at.
  4. Hallstatt salt mine + Skywalk reopen status — currently closed through summer 2026 with "target June reopen, no firm date" as of 2026-05-17. Re-verify via salzwelten.at. If still closed, Berchtesgaden Salt Mine is queued as the substitute.
  5. Berchtesgaden Salt Mine July 2026 hours — the Hallstatt substitute. Confirm if you'll need it.
  6. Wider road / cog / cable car closures — quick spot-check on Jennerbahn, Untersbergbahn, and the Grossglockner Hochalpenstrasse to make sure none are shut for July maintenance.
  7. Car rental DiscoverCars Full Coverage exclusions — re-read the policy before clicking book. Insurance terms can shift. See the rental car page for the current flagged gotchas.

Why this list and not "re-verify everything": lodging + map coords + free-cancellation + photos were verified May 17. Re-checking all of that in late July adds noise without changing decisions. These 7 are the only items where late-breaking changes break the trip's locked moments.
