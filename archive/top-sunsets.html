5 sunsets that will steal this trip.

Sunsets are sacred. Five spots on this trip that earn the front-row seat — re-ranked for the v4 bases (Salzburg / Zell am See / Gosau / Airport). Where to stand, what time, last-cable-car-back, what to do before the sky lights.

5 ranked sunset spots for the 7 nights. Vorderer Gosausee mirror · Schmittenhöhe peak above Zell · Hallstatt Markt · Krippenstein 5 Fingers (day-trip with descent caveat) · Mönchsberg ridge in Salzburg. Each card: where to stand, what time, last-cable-car-back, what to do before sky lights.

How these picks were chosen (v4 re-rank)

  • Re-ranked 2026-05-19 around the 4 actual bases (Salzburg / Zell am See / Gosau / Airport). The previous ranking centered on the Schafbergspitze summit-overnight concept — that's gone. The new #1 (Gosausee mirror) wins on access: 5-min walk from base, no transit dependency, full Dachstein-reflection geometry.
  • Variety filter: one walk-from-base mirror lake (Gosausee), one cable-car alpine peak (Schmittenhöhe above Zell), one painted-village lake-shore (Hallstatt Markt day-trip), one cantilever-platform day-trip (Krippenstein 5 Fingers — with descent caveat), one ridge in Salzburg (Mönchsberg, Shabbat-legal walk-up). Different angles, different moods.
  • Descent risk flagged. Cable-car + cog sunsets need the last-down timetable cross-checked for 2026 evening rides. Krippenstein 5 Fingers especially: last gondola down is 19:10 most evenings, sunset 20:48 means you watch the light go BEFORE actual sun-touch-horizon if you obey the last-gondola rule.
  • Sunset times below are for the specific trip night each spot is best — pulled from timeanddate.com — Salzburg and the Itinerary page anchors. Re-verify week-of in case of schedule shifts.

Couldn't verify (fail-loud): Schmittenhöhe + Krippenstein cable-car 2026 evening timetables — re-verify on schmitten.at / dachstein-salzkammergut.com before counting on any specific descent slot. Schafbergbahn cog usually stops ~19:30 (not 21:15 as previously listed) — sunset 20:48 means it's a day-trip spot, not a stay-late spot. Photo of the Mönchsberg pick uses the Festung Hohensalzburg Wikimedia shot — closest match for the angle from the ridge, actual silhouette looking west toward Untersberg will differ.
