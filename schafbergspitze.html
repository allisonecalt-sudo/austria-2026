The 7 steps, in order

1. Book the hotel (today)
Call +43 (0)6138 3542 during Austria office hours (08:30-12:00 or 16:00-20:00 CET). Israel is 1 hour ahead = call between 09:30-13:00 OR 17:00-21:00 your time. Ask for Wed Jul 29 → Thu Jul 30, 2 adults, westerly window. Confirm cog ticket included.
2. Pack a small overnight bag
ONE small overnight bag per person. Sleeping clothes, fresh layer for sunrise (5°C temp drop at 1,783m vs valley), camera. Leave main suitcases in the car at the valley station (free overnight parking for hotel guests). Cog luggage capacity is small — no full suitcases.
3. Pack kosher dinner + breakfast
Hotel restaurant: hot kitchen until 20:00, NOT kosher. No in-room kitchen, no microwave. Bring your own meal — sealed kosher meat (Vienna Va'ad or Israel-brought), wine, breakfast for the morning. Pack a small thermos for hot drinks. They don't restrict outside food.
4. Drive Obertraun → St. Wolfgang (Wed afternoon)
Leave the Obertraun apartment by 13:30 at the latest. Drive ~1h30 to St. Wolfgang (the valley station town). Quick lakeside walk + lunch picnic en route if time. Park at Parkplatz Nr. 7 (signposted, near the cog station, FREE for overnight guests). Google Maps directions →
5. Board the 16:30 cog (BY 16:00)
Walk from Parkplatz Nr. 7 to the valley station — <5 min. Check in at the ticket counter ("Hausgäste / overnight guests"). Show booking confirmation. Board the cog by 16:25 latest. 40-minute ride up the mountain, gaining 1,500m. The cog is gentle, views are huge.
6. Check in + sunset (17:15 onwards)
Arrive summit ~17:10. Check into Berghotel Schafbergspitze. Room with shower + WC. Hot kitchen at the hotel restaurant until 20:00 (or eat your own kosher meal in the room). Sunset Wed Jul 29: ~20:50. Views west over Lake Wolfgangsee + 13 surrounding lakes. After the last day-tripper cog departs (~18:35 typical), the summit empties out and it's just overnight guests.
7. Sunrise + descent (Thu morning)
Sunrise Thu Jul 30: ~05:30. Worth setting an alarm — gold light on the Dachstein. Hotel breakfast or your own. Take the first cog DOWN (typically ~09:00, confirm at check-in). Drive ~1h30 from St. Wolfgang to the airport-area apartment (last night before the Fri morning flight).

What can go wrong

Miss the 16:30 cog. If you arrive at 16:35, you cannot ride up as an overnight guest. The remaining day-tripper cogs go up and back, NOT one-way. Fix: leave Obertraun by 13:30 even if it feels early. Padding > missing the moment.
Weather closes the cog. High winds or storms can close the Schafbergbahn last-minute. The hotel will refund or rebook. Have a Plan B for sleeping — if cog is down, you can sleep at a lakeside apartment in St. Wolfgang (call ahead for same-day) or drive back to Obertraun and lose the summit night entirely.
Low cloud at sunset. Even if cog runs, summit can be in cloud. Nothing to do but accept it — the cog up + the room + the morning still happen. Sunrise is more reliable than sunset for clarity in July (less afternoon convective cloud build-up).
Forgetting the kosher dinner. No kosher option on the mountain. If you arrive without packed food, you're eating room snacks. Pre-pack at the Obertraun apartment Wed morning.
