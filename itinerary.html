Optional · shared interest

Jewish Salzburg — places worth a stop

All walkable from the Salzburg apartment. Pick 1 or 2 — top picks starred. Mauthausen is last only because it's a full day.

★ Recommended first

Stolpersteine route on Linzergasse

Brass cobblestones for Jews deported from this street. Passes the apartment + Chabad. Free, light load, deeply moving. 20-90 min depending on depth. Easiest "yes."

★ Recommended first

Judengasse + IKG courtyard

Medieval Jewish quarter + Kristallnacht memorial, walkable from the apartment. 1-2 hours together. Pairs cleanly with Stolpersteine for a Sunday afternoon Jewish-Salzburg loop.

Full list — synagogue, cemetery, museum, more

IKG Salzburg synagogue (Lasserstraße 8 — call ahead), Jewish cemetery on the Kommunalfriedhof, plus walking-tour operators + a small reading list.

Heavy · full day

Mauthausen memorial — if you commit a full day

~1.5h drive from Salzburg. Emotionally heavy. A real commitment of energy + time. You both decide whether the day is right for it — no pressure either way.
